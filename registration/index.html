  • Student Registration: $350.00

How to register:

Registration is a three part process. You need to create an account, you need to register, and separately you can pay for your registration. You can register separately for the tutorials and the conference.

SBP-BRiMS 2017 has two types of scholarships available: a diversity scholarship and a student scholarship. You can apply for either or both awards. However, you can only receive one award. DEADLINE TO APPLY FOR SBP-BRIMS'16 TRAVEL SUPPORT IS MAY 16, 2017. If you are applying for a scholarship, register for the conference and tutorials of interest to confirm your registration but wait to pay the registration fee until after the fellowships have been awarded

Diversity Scholarship

Amount: Determined based on need. Scholarships are based on actual expenses and are not a fixed amount. The maximum that can be received per person is $800.

The goal of the diversity scholarship is to help promote involvement by diverse groups. The successful applicant can apply the scholarship to pay for conference fee, tutorials, and/or travel. Preference is given to those students who are presenting papers or posters, or taking part in the challenge problem.

Student Scholarship

Amount: Determined based on need. Scholarships are based on actual expenses and are not a fixed amount. The maximum that can be received per person is $800.

The goal of the student scholarship is to help promote increased attendance by college students. The successful applicant can apply the scholarship to pay for conference fee, tutorials, and/or travel. Preference is given to those students who are presenting papers or posters, or taking part in the challenge problem.
