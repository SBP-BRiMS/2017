2017 International Conference on Social Computing, Behavioral-Cultural Modeling & Prediction and Behavior Representation in Modeling and Simulation
July 5-8, 2017, George Washington University, Washington DC, USA

The 2017 International Conference on Social Computing, Behavioral-Cultural Modeling, and Prediction (SBP-BRiMS) 2017 will be held in Washington, D.C. at George Washington University, 1922 F Street, NW Washington, DC 20052, with the Foggy Bottom/GWU Metro stop on the Orange, Blue and Silver lines located on campus.

Parking: If you're driving to campus, you can enter the Marvin Center's address of 800 21st Street NW into your GPS as a reference point or head right to one of our visitor parking garages. Be sure to review the parking garage rates and hours before you arrive.
