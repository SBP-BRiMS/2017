2017 International Conference on Social Computing, Behavioral-Cultural Modeling & Prediction and Behavior Representation in Modeling and Simulation
July 5-8, 2017, Lehman Auditorium, George Washington University, Washington DC, USA

The 2017 International Conference on Social Computing, Behavioral-Cultural Modeling, and Prediction (SBP-BRiMS) 2017 will be held in Washington, D.C. at George Washington University, 1922 F Street, NW Washington, DC 20052, with the Foggy Bottom/GWU Metro stop on the Orange, Blue and Silver lines located on campus.

George Washington University is home to a number of special programs related to the topics covered at the SBP-BRiMS Conference. These programs include the Department of Engineering Management and Systems Engineering ( www.emse.seas.gwu.edu/), the School of Engineering and Applied Science ( www.seas.gwu.edu/), and the Master of Science in Data Analytics ( graduate.seas.gwu.edu/ms-data-analytics).

Parking: If you're driving to campus, you can enter the Marvin Center's address of 800 21st Street NW into your GPS as a reference point or head right to one of the visitor parking garages. Be sure to review the parking garage rates and hours before you arrive.
